Our Services

Topographical Survey

Comprehensive representation of land features and elevations using advanced tools like Total Station, GPS, and Drone.

Features:
  • Measurement of ground levels, slopes, and contours
  • Identification of natural and man-made features
  • Preparation of digital maps, CAD drawings, and 3D models
  • Accurate site data for planning, design, and engineering
Benefits:
  • Complete understanding of land conditions
  • Optimal infrastructure & drainage planning
  • Reduces errors, delays, and costs
  • Earthwork estimation & land grading
  • Legal & regulatory compliance
  • Informed decision-making for developers & engineers
Boundary Survey

Accurate property boundary identification and demarcation using digital instruments.

Benefits:
  • Prevents land disputes
  • Accurate verification of records & title documents
  • Supports land registration & fencing
  • Clear boundary marking for construction
Building Marking

Precise marking of construction points as per approved plans using advanced digital tools.

Services Include:
  • Excavation & foundation marking
  • Column center line & grid marking
  • Plinth, floor & reference level transfer
  • Alignment & orientation checking
  • Digital verification for construction accuracy
Benefits:
  • High accuracy & reduced errors
  • Saves time & avoids costly rework
  • Ensures construction as per drawings
  • Improves structural alignment & quality
Contour Survey

Accurate mapping of land elevations, depressions, and slopes.

Benefits:
  • Clear understanding of land levels & slopes
  • Essential for drainage & water flow planning
  • Reduces earthwork costs
  • Minimizes construction errors & rework
